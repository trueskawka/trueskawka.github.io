RC - day 23

Achievements:

  • check-in
  • keeping the meditation habit streak
  • warm-up in Python
  • pairing with Juan on Jekyll plugin
  • added subpages for every date and improved calendar
  • worked on presentation
  • started writing a GitBook on Jekyll
  • slowly getting addicted to podcasts

This week I’ve switched to working mostly with Jekyll, as the blog and website turned out to be a pretty interesting side project. I’ve started writing a GitBook on Jekyll (or more accurately - I wrote a Readme draft), as a byproduct of my learning efforts, as it may be useful to others.

I’ve had a chance to pair with Juan and understand better how Jekyll plugins work. Together we’ve created a plugin for generating a subpage for every category and date. I then implemented them in my calendar - now you can click on any date that has posts and be redirected to an appropriate subpage.

I also worked on my presentation, as 5 minutes needs far more work than a half-hour format, which I’m generally used to. I’m keeping it fun and simple, hopefully it will be interesting enough.

Podcasts are great, I’m listening to them whenever I’m not working. The thread on Zulip grew pretty fast and now I have far more recommendations than I could hope to check in the next month, but that just makes it a challenge, doesn’t it? So far I’ve only listened to all You are not so smart episodes on logical fallacies and some Partially Examined Life, but the list is slowly growing.
