RC - day 27

Achievements:

  • self check-in
  • keeping the meditation habit streak
  • warm-up in Ruby
  • building a simple 52/17 timer
  • reading random books
  • staying positive

Plague prevailed and spread - there was no Internet today neither at RC, nor at the Airbnb. Funny how easy it is to forget how often I use it. And how safe it makes me feel. With the ability to contact my loved ones any time I want, I haven’t really felt far from home up till now.

I decided to make an exercise on patience out of it. I’ve been doing everything I could without Internet, untilI really needed it - mostly to check my email and organise a new apartment.

Lessons learned:

  • share your grapes (literal grapes, when you buy 2+ lbs)
  • pick up random books that look fun
  • add pumpkin spice to your morning coffee before you pour hot water in
  • breathe
  • if someone offers to share their data plan - use it for the sake of your sanity (thank you V!)
  • chocolate soothes all stress

I’ve created a simple 52/17 timer (52 minutes for work, 17 minutes playtime), since Fokasu has irritating advertisements and it was a good exercise on programming without Internet. Never thought simple JavaScript can take so much time.
