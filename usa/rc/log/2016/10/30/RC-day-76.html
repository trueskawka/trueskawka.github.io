RC - day 76

Achievements:

  • bought some gifts for friends and family
  • went for Korean BBQ
  • had a nice, long chat with Luna

I’ve finally bought some gifts - I’ve been here for so long, I completely forgot it’s time to fly to Atlanta soon and I haven’t done the shopping yet. I usually avoid walking up Broadway and lurking into the shops, but this time it was a blessing. I’ve checked nearly half of my list.

Afterwards we went for a great Korean BBQ evening with Cihan, Joe and Miguel. This time I was wise enough to not drink soju, as it is a sneaky beverage. One honey ice cream dessert later I headed back to RC to do some work.

I’ve also had a lovely chat with Luna, one of the friends I’ll miss most when leaving New York. We tend to have long conversations that run for hours and we’ve only noticed it was time to go home around 5.30am. That’s what RC does to people.
